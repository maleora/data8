Blood Junction

When journalist India Kane travels from Sydney to the outback town of Cooinda for a reunion with her friend Lauren, she has no idea of the town's appalling history. There, forty years earlier, an entire Amoriginal family had been massacred. Locals renamed the town Blood Junction.Picked up by an off-duty cop when her car dies in route, India arrives in Cooinda to find that Lauren has disappeared. Next day...
